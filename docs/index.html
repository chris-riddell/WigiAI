What is WigiAI?

WigiAI brings AI companions to your macOS desktop as draggable widgets that live alongside your work. Unlike chat apps that hide in tabs or menus, your AI assistant is always visible and accessible. Track habits naturally through conversation, get personalized reminders with celebration animations, and build better routines with AI that remembers your context.

üí≠ Why I Built This

I wanted an AI assistant that felt more like a companion than a tool. Existing AI apps either hide in browser tabs or lack the habit tracking features I needed. I kept forgetting to check my habit tracker, and my AI conversations felt disconnected from my actual goals.

WigiAI combines the best of both worlds:

  • Visible desktop widgets - Your AI is always present, not hidden away
  • Conversational habit tracking - Just chat instead of clicking checkboxes
  • Persistent memory - AI remembers your context across all conversations
  • Privacy-first design - Offline voice features and local AI support (Ollama)

üöÄ Quick Start

  1. Download the latest release from GitHub
  2. Install to /Applications and run
  3. Add API key (OpenAI, Ollama, or other compatible service)
  4. Choose a character from the library or create your own
  5. Start chatting - your AI companion is ready!
